Just a simple video-looping application utilising Youtube's IFrame API.
Currently accepts URLs which extend Youtube's syntax slightly:
?v={video id}&t={time of start}&t1={time of end} (random-ish example)
To move outside the marked region, e.g. to mark a new one, you need to either pause or disable looping first.
To share your settings, just copy the current URL.
